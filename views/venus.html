Facts

Venus is the second brightest (natural) object in the sky behind the moon.

It is possible that billions of years ago the climate of Venus was similar to that of Earth.

Venus rotates in the opposite direction of all of the other planets.

The average surface temperature of Venus is 462 degrees Celsius

The atmospheric pressue of Venus is 92 times stronger than Earth's.
